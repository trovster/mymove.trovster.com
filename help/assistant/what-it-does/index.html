Assistant - What It Does

In order to rate a house, please click on the title bar.

If you want to remove all properties, please click on "remove all".

If you want to remove one certain house, please click on the title bar and thereafter "delete" at the bottom.

If a house is rated, the empty bar will fill and display an exact percentage value. Please notice that the percentage value is just a rough estimation of the quality of a house. The word quality is defined in terms of lifestyle, cleanness, safety and environment of the property. In any case a house inspection is required before signing a tenancy contract.
