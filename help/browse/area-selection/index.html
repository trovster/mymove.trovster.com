In order to select an area, please click on the correct number or name in the list below.

The map shows a 3D model of the different districts around the University of Bradford, which are suitable for student housing.

The five areas are encoded with a colour code. Thereby dark green indicates closeness to the city centre whereas dark blue signifies the direction city outwards.
